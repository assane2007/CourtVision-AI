# 🏀 CourtVision AI — The Ultimate Elite Sports Ecosystem

CourtVision AI is no longer just a tracking app. It is a full-scale, futuristic basketball ecosystem designed for the next generation of athletes. 

## 🌌 Revolutionary Features (v2.0)

### 👻 Ghost Mode (Digital Twin AR)
Compare your shooting mechanics in real-time or post-game against your "Perfect Form" ghost model. Built with advanced skeletal tracking to identify every degree of elbow flare or jump height deficiency.

### 🎬 Cinematic AI Highlights
Generate share-ready TikTok/Instagram reels instantly. Our "Iron Man" HUD overlays automatically detect your best plays and wrap them in a biometric, glitch-effect visualization that wows your followers.

### 🕹️ The Live Playbook
Gamify your training. Receive real-time AI objectives on your camera screen (e.g., "Hit 5 catch-and-shoot 3s"). Watch your progress bars fill up as you dominate the court.

### 🥊 The Shadow League
Your "Digital Twin" never sleeps. Every night, our multi-agent simulation engine pits your stats against rivals in 1,000 simulated games. Wake up to a **Morning Report** from your AI Coach explaining your tactical edge.

### 🎥 3D Gaussian Reconstruction
Turn a single 2D video into a fully navigatable 3D scene. Re-watch your game from any angle using our GPU-cloud powered reconstruction pipeline.

## 🏗️ Monorepo Structure

- **[apps/mobile](apps/mobile/)** : React Native (Expo) - The Dark Arena UI.
- **[packages/api](packages/api/)** : Fastify Backend - The Async Engine (BullMQ + Redis).
- **[packages/ai](packages/ai/)** : Computer Vision Pipeline - 7-Step Analysis.

## 🚀 Quick Start

1. **Install**: `npm install`
2. **Infra**: `docker-compose up -d` (Requires Redis for the Shadow League)
3. **Run**: `npm run dev:api` | `npx expo start`
